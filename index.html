About

The Panda, native to south central China, is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body. Though it belongs to the order Carnivora, the panda's diet is over 99% bamboo.

In the wild, they are solitary animals. They spend most of their time eating and resting, living a life of quiet contemplation in the misty mountains.
